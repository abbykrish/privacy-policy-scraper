Effective Date:  August 5, 2019  Privacy Notice: California Residents    Scope of this Policy  AmRent, Inc. has created this privacy policy in order to describe how we collect, use, maintain, share, and protect your personal information when you use our website, www.amrent.com. This privacy policy applies to information collected when you visit our website. It does not apply to information collected in any other way, including information collected offline.    Children's Privacy  Our Website is not directed at children under the age of 13.    Changes to this Policy  We may modify this privacy policy at any time. If we do, we will post the revised version here. You should periodically check here for the most up-to-date version of this privacy policy.    Information Automatically Collected  When you visit the website, we automatically collect certain information, like your IP address, device ID, browser type and operating system, the domain from which you came, the page you are viewing, and the date and time of your visit.    We use this information for a number of purposes, such as investigating security incidents, running analytics to understand usage, and for compliance with security standards.    Personally Information  You may elect to share personal information with us by filling out the information on our Contact Us page, such as name, email address, phone number, and any other personal information you choose to provide. We use information you send to us through the Contact Us page to respond to your inquiries or comments.    If you choose to apply for a job through our website, you may enter personal information such as name, address, email address, social security number, signature, telephone number, education, employment, employment history, and citizenship status. We use this information to employment purposes.    Google Analytics  We use a tool called “Google Analytics” to collect information about use of this site, including IP address, geolocation, language, the domain from which you came, and browser type. Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to collect information such as how often users visit this site, what pages they visit when they do so, and what other sites they used prior to coming to this site. We do not combine the information collected through the use of Google Analytics with personal information. For information on how Google Analytics uses data and how to control information sent to Google, visit www.google.com/policies/privacy/partners/ .    We use the information we get from Google Analytics to understand how our website is used and make improvements.    Cookies  We use cookies to collect website usage data, to improve the site and our services, as well as your experience with the site. Cookies are pieces of information generated by a web server and stored in your computer. AmRent only uses session cookies. A session cookie is deleted when you close the browser. You can control cookies through your browser settings and other tools.    Third-Party Links and Websites  You may be able to access third-party sites by clicking links on our site. We are not responsible for the privacy policies or practices of any third-party sites, and if you visit those websites, we encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.    Choice & Online Tracking  As a user of our website, you control whether to send us personal information by using our Contact Us form.    AmRent does not track your online activities over time and across third party websites. However, other third parties may be conducting online tracking on our site. To learn more about the choices you have regarding online tracking, please visit these sites:  http://www.aboutads.info/choices/  http://www.youradchoices.com    Information Sharing and Disclosure  We don’t intend to share your information with third parties. Privacy Practices  AmRent's policies and procedures for preparing and processing of investigative consumer reports are designed to comply with the requirements of the California Investigative Consumer Reporting Agencies Act (Cal. Civ. Code § 1786 et seq.) and the federal Fair Credit Reporting Act (15 U.S.C. § 1681 et seq.). AmRent, for example, maintains policies and procedures designed to limit the purposes for, and circumstances under which, we furnish investigative consumer reports. AmRent provides individuals with the ability to request, upon proper identification, access to information we hold about them in our investigative consumer reporting files. AmRent conducts reinvestigations of the accuracy and completeness of information at the individual's request. AmRent also has implemented administrative, physical and technical safeguards designed to protect the information that we maintain in our investigative consumer reporting files.
